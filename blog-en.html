this website was designed as a personal portfolio. i wanted to create a minimalist space where i can share my projects, thoughts and works .

instagram @technovision101

I produce content on Instagram by combining my own perception of beauty and aesthetic values with computer science. In particular, I talk about people who have made significant contributions in this field under the name of the "IconTech" series. Other posts focus on events, tools and inventions that have revolutionary qualities around the world.

You can see some of my posts below and click on them directly. As the name suggests, this page was created to provide a vision for technology.

Everything you see here has a purpose. There is nothing unnecessary. Simplicity, symmetry and clarity in design are important to me. Because the best works are the ones where the labor spent in the background is not visible. That's why smooth transitions, transparent boxes, golden ratios and symmetries are everywhere.

You are invited to visit my Instagram page to access all my posts and see the wonders of science.
