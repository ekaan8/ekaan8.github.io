hi, emre kaan is here

This site exists to share the ideas, projects and dreams that are floating around in my mind. I am a computer engineering student, but beyond technical knowledge, what excites me the most is art itself. I believe that art should drive technique, not the other way around. Here you will see sometimes my thoughts and sometimes how I blend art and technology. I believe that beauty and aesthetics are not just an addition, but a starting point. I'm planning to share the content I share on social media here. I hope something here will inspire you too.
