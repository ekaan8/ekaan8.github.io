This site exists to share the ideas, projects and dreams that are floating around in my mind. I am a computer engineering student, but beyond technical knowledge, what excites me the most is art itself. I believe that art should drive technique, not the other way around. I believe that beauty and aesthetics are not just an addition, but a starting point. I plan to share the content I share on social media here. I hope something here will inspire you too.

content creation @technovision101

I produce content on Instagram by combining my own perception of beauty and aesthetic values with computer science. In particular, I talk about people who have made significant contributions in this field under the name of the "IconTech" series. Other posts focus on events, tools and inventions that have revolutionary qualities around the world. You can see some of my posts below and click on them directly. As the name suggests, this page was created to provide a vision for technology. Everything you see here has a purpose. There is nothing unnecessary. Simplicity, symmetry and clarity in design are important to me. Because the best works are the ones where the labor spent in the background is not visible. That's why smooth transitions, transparent boxes, golden ratios and symmetries are everywhere. You are invited to visit my instagram page to access all my posts and see the wonders of science.
